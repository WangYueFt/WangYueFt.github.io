I am an Assistant Professor at [USC CS](https://www.cs.usc.edu) (2023 Fall-), leading the [Physical Superintelligence Lab](https://psi-lab.ai). I graduated from MIT EECS in 2022, advised by [Prof. Justin Solomon](https://people.csail.mit.edu/jsolomon) at the [Geometric Data Processing Group](https://groups.csail.mit.edu/gdpgroup). Previously, I was a master's student at the University of California, San Diego. Prior to that, I received my BEng in Computer Science from Zhejiang University.
